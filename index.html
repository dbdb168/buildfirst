The Spectrum
Four ways people engage with AI.
Anthropic calls them Automation, Augmentation, and Agency. We add a fourth, Asking, because that's where most people actually start. The question isn't where you are. It's what you do next.

Asking

Using AI like a search engine. Questions and answers. Helpful, but you're barely scratching the surface.

Automation

AI handles specific tasks you've defined. Email summaries, template drafts. Repeatable and useful, but you're not learning.

Augmentation

You and AI think together. You iterate, challenge, build. Your judgment plus its capability. This is where the real leverage lives.

Agency

AI handles entire workflows on your behalf. You design the system, review the outputs. The frontier.
