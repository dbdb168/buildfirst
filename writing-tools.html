Diagnostic Tool

Groundwork

Groundwork is a thinking tool for leaders — a plugin that helps you work out where AI could actually be useful in your organisation, then decide which opportunities to start with. It shows you what you'd need to learn before you commit, drawing on Steve Blank's and Alex Osterwalder's lean experimentation methods to turn a long list into real decisions: what to build, what to test first, and — the part that matters most — where to put your investment.

Operating System

IntelligenceOS

Inspired by Jack Dorsey's From Hierarchy to Intelligence, I'm building a secure, agentic operating system for BuildFirst that automates or augments every part of what I do as its founder and solo operator. It self-improves with feedback from me and the world, drawing on pioneers in agentic coding and agent design. IntelligenceOS is a repo of the frameworks, folder structures and agents I've built to run BuildFirst — open source and compiled for you to customise for your own company or your clients.
